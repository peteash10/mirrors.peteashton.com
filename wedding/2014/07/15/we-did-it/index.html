We did it!

July 15, 2014UncategorizedPete Ashton

We’re married! Phew!

We’re going to turn this website into some kind of memorial / celebration over the summer but right now we have a honeymoon to go on.

Meanwhile, thanks everyone! We’re overwhelmed with the good wishes we’ve had. It’s quite humbling!

Photos and Videos!

Many of you were taking photos and we’ve seen a few of them up on the internets. Could you send us copies? It doesn’t matter if you think they’re not good enough – we want to try and see the day from your perspective as well as the official photographers and make a nice big collective album.

We also forgot to video the speeches, which is a tragedy as Michael’s was amazing! If you had the forethought to point your cameraphone at any of that we’d REALLY like copies.

If you don’t have a preferred way to send large files we recommend wetransfer.com which is very simple to use. Send stuff to peteashton@gmail.com
